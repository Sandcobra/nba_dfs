How to read this:
Positive correlation = players whose DFS scores move together — stack them in the same lineup (PG who feeds a C is a classic +0.6 pair).
Negative correlation = usage competition — avoid putting both in the same lineup (two ball-dominant guards on the same team fight for shots).
On/Off impact: When a player is OUT, players negatively correlated with them are the strongest late-swap targets — they absorb the usage.
